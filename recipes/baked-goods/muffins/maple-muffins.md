# Brown Sugar Bran Muffins

## Ingredients
240g flour
200g maple syrup 
10g baking powder
1/4 t salt
1 egg
175g buttermilk
113g oil
Maple chips
1 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp maple extract

## Directions
375F convection for 25
Makes 6 x 2/3c muffins
