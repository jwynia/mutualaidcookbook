# Brown Sugar Bran Muffins

## Original
### Ingredients
- 240g flour
- 200g maple syrup 
- 10g baking powder
- 1/4 t salt
 -1 egg
- 175g buttermilk
- 113g oil
- Maple chips
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp maple extract

### Directions
Bake at 375F convection or 400F conventional for 25 minutes until toothpick comes out clean
Makes 6 x 2/3c muffins
